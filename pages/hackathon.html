#codeFest19

CodeFest is here!


The hackathon is meant for the community. GDG Siliguri is a relatively new community in this ecosystem. As a part of this initiative, we are putting up a hackathon for the participants to build for the community. You will be asked to build modules or tools that will be beneficial or used by the community.

These may include but not limited to,
-Design ideas and implementation of the community web-sites and application(s)
-Building API(s) for communication from application to various messaging platforms like Discord
-Chat-bot for quick queries
-Platform for publishing blogs / articles / news
-Platform for quick broadcasting of upcoming events / announcements
-App development for the community
-Platform for pitching raw ideas


You have to follow few steps to get the invitation and if you are participation in CodeFest you don't have to register separately for the keynotes.

Stages

Stage 1: Team selection and shortlisting

We can accommodate a maximum of 20 teams for the hackathon. Thus, to participate in the hackathon, we will have a small technical coding session where we will ask you to work on a problem which will be quite simple. Based on your approach, the organising team will decide the top 60 participants. The teams will be randomly selected.


Stage 2: Hackathon day -- Yayy!!!

The launch arena has been set, The spark will be ignited by the judging panel to launch the hackathon. You will be working with your team of 3 team-mates, from morning till evening. At the end of the hackathon, we will push all of the code-base to GDG Siliguri’s github page. The winners will get amazing prizes.

Make sure each team has at-least 1 laptop

Prizes and Perks

A hackathon is no good without amazing prizes we receive in the form of experience. We are building for the community so any contribution is a prize. But to keep the competitive spirit alive, here are some amazing prizes we as a community will present to those who stand-out in the community.

1st prize: Google home mini smart speaker
2nd prize: Mi band 3
3rd prize: boAt Rockerz 255 bluetooth headset

Also if you can secure one of these spots, we shall push your creation and showcase it to official GDG Siliguri Repository, and every participants will get special SWAGs from Google.
